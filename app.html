Prof.P.Chandra Sekhar

I am the Principal of University College of Engineering(A), Osmania University and also serve as a Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Nagpur University-1991, Master's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from JNTUH-1999 and received my PhD from the Osmania University-2009. I recived Post-Doctoral Fellowship Scholar at the Shizuoka University, Japan.

My research interests lie in Digital Systems and Computer Electronics, Digital and Analog VLSI.

Ministery of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India sanctioned a prestigious Chip to Start-up (C2S) program worth of 3 Crores to tape out Novel ADPLL IP Core for BLE Applications
